About This Series

I dropped out of Cal Poly's computer science program in 2009 after failing most of my classes freshman year. Spent the next 15 years working, coaching, dancing, and trying different approaches to education. Came back in 2024 with a different understanding of how I learn.

This series documents that journey and what changed.
